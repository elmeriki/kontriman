What is Kontri Man?

Kontri Man is a mobile app that allows people to save money daily and cash out at the end of every month operates through a mechanism commonly known as a "Acaoo", "digital piggy bank" or "savings vault." The benefits of daily saving extend beyond the financial aspect. It instills a sense of accomplishment, as we witness our savings grow over time:

Flexibility and Control

Kontri Man provides users with flexibility and control over their savings. They can adjust their daily savings amount, pause or resume automated savings, and even withdraw funds from their savings vault before the end of the month if necessary.

The daily savings and cash-out feature of Kontri Wallet encourages a disciplined approach to saving money, making it easier for individuals to save small amounts consistently. By automating the process and providing a user-friendly interface, Kontri Man aims to help users achieve their financial goals more effectively.

Cash-Out at the End of the Month: At the end of the month, users have the option to cash out their accumulated savings from the vault. The total savings amount, including any earned interest, is made available for withdrawal or transfer to the user's linked bank account.

With Kontri Man, you can easily set daily savings targets and track your progress over time. Our intuitive interface allows you to customize your savings plan according to your financial aspirations and preferences. Whether you're saving for a dream vacation, a down payment on a home, or creating an emergency fund, Kontri Wallet has got you covered.
