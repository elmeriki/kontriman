What is Kontri Man Wallet?

Kontri Man Wallet is a user-friendly mobile application designed to make daily saving effortless and rewarding. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their finances, cultivate healthy saving habits, and achieve their financial goals.

At Kontriman Cooperative Credit Union, we believe that daily saving is the key to unlocking a brighter financial future. We understand the challenges of managing your finances and saving effectively in today's fast-paced world. That's why we have developed an innovative platform to simplify and streamline your daily saving journey.
